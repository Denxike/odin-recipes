    1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

    Step 1:Mix 2 cups flour and salt together in a bowl. Slowly mix in enough water to make a thick dough. Mix in oil until combined. "
    Step 2"Knead dough on a cool surface for a few minutes, adding a few spoonfuls of flour. Return dough to the bowl, cover with a clean cloth, and let rest for 30 minutes. "
    Step 3"Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F (95 degrees C). "
    Step 4"Divide dough into orange-sized balls. Flatten into 6-inch circles. "
    Step 5"Fry in batches in the hot skillet, turning once, until golden brown and spotted, 6 to 8 minutes. Repeat with remaining dough. Keep chapatis warm in the oven. "
